Organizer Help & FAQ

How do payments work?

You receive 50% advance at booking and remaining 50% after successful event completion.

What if client cancels?

If the client cancels, the 50% advance payment is non-refundable and you can retain it.

Can clients reschedule?

Yes, clients can reschedule the event one time without any penalty.

How to contact clients?

You can directly communicate using the contact details provided in the booking.

Admin fee & payment?

You need to pay 20% admin commission after event completion via the option in the Bookings section.
