Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event?

Browse organizers, select your preferred one, choose date & time, and confirm booking by paying 50% advance.

How does payment work?

You pay 50% during booking and the remaining 50% after successful event completion.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, but the 50% advance payment is non-refundable if you cancel the event.

Can I reschedule my event?

Yes, you can reschedule your event one time without any penalty.

How do I contact the organizer?

You can communicate directly using the organizer's contact details provided after booking.
